Need help with Barrtar?

For closed testing, account, privacy, deletion, safety, or support questions, email Barrtar support. If your question is about an app account, include only the minimum details needed to find the issue.

support@barrtar.com

What to include

  • Your registered phone number only if the request is account-specific.
  • The task title or short task context if the issue is task-specific.
  • Screenshots only when they help explain the problem.
  • Do not email OTPs, passwords, full identity numbers, or unnecessary documents.

For urgent safety concerns

Use in-app reporting when available so the task/user context is attached. If you cannot access the app, email support with the subject "Safety report" and the minimum details needed to review.
