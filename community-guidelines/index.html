Community guidelines

Keep Barrtar safe, lawful, and useful.

Barrtar is for everyday local help. These guidelines explain what is allowed, what is not allowed, and how reports are handled.

Allowed tasks

Use Barrtar for lawful, everyday tasks where the creator and doer can clearly agree the scope, location, timing, price, and proof of completion.

Not allowed

Illegal, unsafe, or exploitative requests.
Weapons, drugs, alcohol, tobacco, or regulated substances.
Sexual services, explicit content, or adult exploitation.
Harassment, stalking, intimidation, hate, or threats.
Tasks involving minors or improper contact with minors.
Professional medical, legal, financial, or emergency services.
Dangerous work without lawful training, equipment, or permission.
Fraud, identity misuse, scams, fake reviews, or academic cheating.

Reporting

Report suspicious users, unsafe tasks, payment issues, harassment, or policy violations in the app or at support@barrtar.com. Include task details and screenshots or proof where safe to do so.

What Barrtar may do

Barrtar may remove tasks, limit task visibility, pause matching, restrict accounts, request additional verification, preserve evidence, or cooperate with lawful requests when needed to protect users and platform integrity.
