Privacy Policy

Simple, clear privacy information for Barrtar.

This Privacy Policy explains how Barrtar collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the Barrtar app, website, waitlist, and support channels.

Last updated May 1, 2026 Contact support@barrtar.com

1. What Barrtar may collect

Depending on how you use Barrtar, we may collect the following kinds of information:

Account and profile information

Phone number, OTP or login data, name, photo, age bracket if you provide it, skills, profile details, ratings, and feedback.

Task and activity information

Task details, task location, schedules, offers, status updates, uploaded documents or images, and dispute or payment proof that you submit.

Verification information

Verification or KYC details, including government ID or other documents needed to verify a user before certain actions.

Device and support information

Approximate or precise location when enabled, device or app diagnostics, crash logs, app version, push notification token, support requests, and idea submissions.

If you contact us through the website or by email, we may also collect your email address and the contents of your message.

2. How we use this information

  • To create and secure your account and verify logins.
  • To show your profile and task information inside the platform.
  • To match task creators and doers, including by location.
  • To verify users and help reduce fraud, abuse, and unsafe activity.
  • To review disputes, complaints, and payment proof submitted by users.
  • To send service updates, notifications, and support replies.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and product decisions.
Important payment note for Barrtar v1 Barrtar v1 does not process platform payments and does not hold user funds. Users settle directly with each other, for example by UPI or cash. If a dispute happens, Barrtar may review the proof users choose to submit.

3. When we may share information

We may share information in limited situations such as:

  • With matched task participants when needed to complete a task or settle direct payment, such as limited profile, task, location context, ratings, and contact/payment coordination details.
  • With service providers who help us operate authentication, OTP delivery, verification/KYC checks, hosting, storage, notifications, analytics, security, and customer support.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect safety, rights, or Barrtar's platform integrity.
  • As part of a merger, restructuring, or business transfer, subject to applicable law.

4. Location, notifications, and permissions

Barrtar may request location access so nearby tasks and matches can work properly. Barrtar may also use a push notification token to send important account, task, and support updates. You can control many permissions from your device settings, but some features may not work fully if permissions are turned off.

5. Data retention and security

We keep information for as long as needed to operate Barrtar, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and maintain business records. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and operational measures to protect data, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

6. Your choices

  • You can review or update some profile details inside Barrtar.
  • You can turn off location or notification permissions in your device settings.
  • You may request account deactivation or deletion support through the app or by contacting us, subject to legal, safety, fraud-prevention, dispute, and record-keeping requirements.
  • You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion support where available.
  • You can choose not to send support requests, feedback, or idea submissions.

7. Children

Barrtar is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page. Continued use of Barrtar after an update means the revised policy will apply.

9. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or Barrtar's data practices, contact us at support@barrtar.com.
