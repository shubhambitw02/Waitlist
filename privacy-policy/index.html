Privacy Policy

Simple, clear privacy information for Barrtar.

This Privacy Policy explains how Barrtar collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the Barrtar app, website, waitlist, verification, support, and local task features.

Last updated: June 2, 2026 Contact: support@barrtar.com

1. What Barrtar may collect

Account and profile

Phone number, login and OTP metadata, name, profile photo, skills, language, gender if collected in the app, addresses, ratings, feedback, and profile details.

Tasks and activity

Task details, task location, timing, price, status changes, offers, reservations, direct-payment proof, completion proof, disputes, reports, and support interactions.

Verification

Identity verification submissions and document images or data needed to verify a doer before they accept and work on tasks.

Device, website, and waitlist

Email for invite requests, app diagnostics, crash logs, version, approximate or precise location when enabled, push notification token, and basic website analytics.

2. How we use information

  • To create accounts, secure logins, and prevent OTP abuse.
  • To show profiles, tasks, task history, notifications, and support state.
  • To match creators and doers by location, availability, timing, and skills.
  • To verify doers before accepting work and reduce unsafe or abusive use.
  • To review reports, disputes, payment proof, and completion proof.
  • To send service updates, task alerts, support replies, and tester communication.
  • To improve reliability, performance, safety, and product decisions.
Barrtar v1 does not process platform payments and does not hold user funds. Users pay each other directly, such as by UPI or cash. Barrtar may review proof that users choose to submit if a dispute or support case happens.

3. When information may be shared

  • With matched task participants when needed to coordinate a task, such as profile, task, location, contact, payment coordination, rating, or proof context.
  • With service providers that help operate OTP delivery, verification/KYC, notifications, hosting, storage, analytics, security, and support.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, user safety, or platform integrity.
  • As part of a lawful business transfer, restructuring, or similar event, subject to applicable law.

4. Permissions and notifications

Barrtar may request location access so nearby tasks, task locations, and matching can work. Barrtar may use notification permission and push tokens to send task, availability, account, and support updates. You can manage many permissions from your device settings.

5. Retention and deletion

We keep information for as long as needed to operate Barrtar, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, preserve evidence where required, and maintain audit or business records.

You can request account deletion in the app or through our account deletion page. Current app deletion flow disables the account immediately, schedules eligible deletion after 24 hours, and may pause deletion for legal, safety, dispute, abuse, assigned-task, active-task, or evidence-preservation reasons.

6. Security

Barrtar uses reasonable technical, administrative, and operational safeguards for user information, including private storage for sensitive uploads and backend-owned security controls. No system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Children

Barrtar is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly allow children or minors to use Barrtar as task creators or doers.

8. Contact us

If you have questions about privacy, data access, correction, or deletion, contact support@barrtar.com.
