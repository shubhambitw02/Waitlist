Terms for using Barrtar.

Barrtar is a peer-to-peer marketplace for everyday local tasks. Creators and doers agree task details and direct payment with each other. Barrtar does not hold user funds in v1 and charges zero platform fees.

1. Who can use Barrtar

You must be old enough to enter a binding agreement in your region and use Barrtar only for lawful, safe, and respectful tasks. Barrtar may verify identity before certain actions, including task acceptance.

2. Direct payments

Barrtar v1 does not process platform payments and does not hold funds. Task participants settle directly, such as through UPI or cash. Users are responsible for agreeing the price, scope, proof of completion, and any lawful taxes or records that apply to them.

3. Prohibited tasks and conduct

Do not post, accept, request, or complete tasks that are illegal, unsafe, exploitative, deceptive, or harmful. This includes:

Illegal goods, services, or activity.
Weapons, drugs, alcohol, tobacco, or regulated substances.
Sexual services, explicit content, or exploitation.
Harassment, threats, stalking, hate, or intimidation.
Work involving minors or attempts to contact minors improperly.
Dangerous work without lawful training, tools, or permissions.
Professional medical, legal, financial, or emergency services.
Fraud, identity misuse, academic cheating, or deceptive reviews.

4. Safety, reports, and enforcement

Users can report unsafe tasks, abusive behavior, or suspicious activity in the app or at support@barrtar.com. Barrtar may remove content, restrict accounts, pause task access, preserve evidence, or cooperate with lawful requests when needed to protect users and platform integrity.

5. Disputes

Because payment is direct between users, Barrtar cannot guarantee payment recovery. If a dispute is raised, Barrtar may review task details, status history, submitted proof, and reports to help mediate a fair resolution or take account action.

6. Account and data requests

Privacy choices are described in our Privacy Policy. Account deletion requests can be started at Account Deletion.

7. Changes

Barrtar may update these Terms as the product changes. Continued use of Barrtar after an update means the updated Terms apply.
