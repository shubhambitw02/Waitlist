Privacy Terms Guidelines
Account deletion

Delete your Barrtar account and eligible personal data.

You can request deletion from inside the Barrtar app or by contacting support from the email or phone number linked to your account.

How to request deletion

  1. Open Barrtar and go to Profile, then Privacy and account, then request account deletion.
  2. If you cannot access the app, email support@barrtar.com with the subject "Account deletion request" from your registered email or include the phone number linked to the account.
  3. We may verify that the request came from the account owner before deleting or deactivating the account.

What gets deleted

Eligible account and profile data, saved preferences, and personal data connected to your Barrtar account are deleted or anonymized after verification, unless retention is needed for the reasons below.

Active tasks and disputes

If your account has active tasks, open disputes, safety reports, or unresolved support cases, we may ask you to close those matters before deletion is completed.

What may be retained

Barrtar may retain limited records where needed for legal obligations, safety, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, enforcement of our terms, audit logs, or legitimate business records. Retained records are kept only as long as needed for those purposes.

Timing

We aim to respond within 7 days and complete eligible deletion within 30 days after verification, unless a longer period is required for legal, safety, fraud-prevention, or dispute reasons.

Questions

Contact support@barrtar.com for account deletion, privacy, or data access questions.
