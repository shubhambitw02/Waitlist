Account deletion

Delete your Barrtar account and eligible personal data.

You can request deletion from inside the Barrtar app. If you cannot access the app, contact support from the phone or email context linked to your account so we can verify ownership.

Last updated: June 2, 2026 Current app flow: 24-hour pending period

How to request deletion

  1. 1 Open Barrtar and go to Profile, then Privacy and account.
  2. 2 Choose account deletion and confirm the request.
  3. 3 If you cannot access the app, email support@barrtar.com with the subject "Account deletion request".

What happens after you request deletion

Barrtar disables the account immediately and schedules eligible deletion for completion after a 24-hour pending period. During that period, you can restore the account before deletion is completed.

If there is an unresolved dispute, abuse report, active assigned task, active created task, legal request, or evidence preservation need, deletion may be paused under a legal or safety hold.

What gets deleted or anonymized

Eligible account, profile, saved preference, session, device, task-facing personal, and privacy-request data may be deleted or anonymized after the pending period, subject to safety, legal, dispute, fraud-prevention, and audit requirements.

What may be retained

Barrtar may retain limited records needed for legal obligations, safety investigations, unresolved disputes, abuse prevention, audit logs, support history, evidence preservation, or legitimate business records. Retained records are limited to the purpose that requires retention.

Questions

Contact support@barrtar.com for account deletion, restoration, privacy, or data access questions.
