How it works

Need help nearby? Or want to earn nearby? Same app.

Barrtar is a peer-to-peer local task platform in closed testing. You can post a task when you need help, or turn on availability and accept nearby tasks when you want to earn.

Post local tasks Earn nearby ₹0 platform fee in v1

For task creators

  1. 1 Describe what you need, choose the location, add details, and set the amount.
  2. 2 Barrtar tries to match nearby available doers first.
  3. 3 If nobody accepts quickly, the task can open to wider nearby discovery.
  4. 4 Before sharing the start OTP, check that the person who arrived matches the accepted doer shown in Barrtar.
  5. 5 If the person does not match or something feels wrong, do not share the OTP. Release, deny, or report the task from the app.
  6. 6 Coordinate directly, complete the task, and pay the doer directly.

For doers

  1. 1 Create your profile and complete verification before accepting work.
  2. 2 Turn on availability for match alerts or browse nearby tasks.
  3. 3 Accept only what fits your time, distance, skills, and comfort.
  4. 4 Complete the task, share proof where needed, and receive payment directly from the creator.

What kind of tasks fit Barrtar?

Standing in a queue
Local errands or shopping help
Pamphlet or event support
Packing, carrying, or shifting help
Shop, business, or practical support
Skill help, learning, or simple guidance

What Barrtar does not do in v1

Barrtar does not charge users a platform fee, does not hold user money, and does not guarantee earnings, payment recovery, task completion, or perfect safety. Barrtar provides discovery, matching, coordination, verification gates, task evidence, and dispute support.
